BCAC About

BCAChain is a blockchain industry application project jointly launched by industry elites in the financial, retail, social e-commerce and other fields and former IBM blockchain laboratory technology members in early 2018, focusing on the new retail market of 100 billion yuan. To build a new retail commercial credit economy based on blockchain. BCAChain first proposed the RAFT consensus and applied KYC and other authentication technologies to accelerate and optimize the application of blockchain. BCAChain is building a new decentralized retail platform with its own private chain technology to help develop blockchain and new retail entities. At present, BCAChain has completed the release of the blockchain low-level code open source and DAPP wallet. On November 8, 2018, the BCAC Foundation officially joined the China Cloud System Industry Innovation Strategic Alliance to become a governing unit.

Product

BCAC business link will fully launch the retail traceability plan, and use blockchain technology, Internet of things technology and big data to track the full link of retail commodities to ensure the integrity of commodity information. And use blockchain technology to strengthen privacy protection of user data and build consensus trust at low cost. Build a big data system to deeply analyze customers' demands and improve shopping experience. Application scenarios include: new retail, personal credit system, enterprise credit system, etc.
